HaptiClock. Feel the time.

Using an Activator action, you can trigger a sequence of tiny vibrations (haptic feedback) that tell you the time. Each digit has as many vibrations as its value (e.g. 5 is 5 vibrations, 3 is 3 etc.). This way, you know how far you are into the day without needing to get your phone out. The best and most inconspicuous way to activate it would be with a hardware action, such as both volume buttons being pressed. You can do that without needing to put your hand in your pocket.
Compatible with iOS 9, 10 and 11 on all devices with haptic feedback. Available in BigBoss and on the Auxilium Development repo.
